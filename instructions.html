HOW TO PLAY

When you see a "glitch" (One of those weird mixed-color rectangles you see around),
If you go into them, you will go into "debug mode!" Be careful, there are software bugs flying around everywhere and if you even try touching them, they will physically harm you!
When an error message pops up, click it as fast as you can to fix it. Good luck!
